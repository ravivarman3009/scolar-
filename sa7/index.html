Audio and video player:

Audio player refers to: Cassette player, a piece of hardware for playing audio cassettes.
CD player, an electronic device that plays audio compact discs. Digital audio player, a
piece of hardware for playing audio files. Audio player (software), a piece of computer
software for playing audio files.

Vdio player refers to:hardware or software that allows for the playback of video. In the
past , most video players were hardware based , like the Beta Max pictured above , but
today , most video players are the soft ware based , running on the phones , browsers,or
computers to play back video content.
