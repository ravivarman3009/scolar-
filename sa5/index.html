My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. Sivakasi
    • Year: 2005
    • Main Actors: Vijay, Prakash Raj, Asin
    • Description: Sivakasi is a welder known for keeping rowdies in check. One day he chastises a jeweller's daughter, Hema, for wearing "skimpy clothing." After she instigates a false case against him, he humiliates her father by declaring his love for her at a wedding. In response, Hema claims he caused her sister's suicide.
  2. Thirupaachi
    • Year: 2005
    • Main Actors:Vijay,Trisha,Pasupathy
    • Description:Sivagiri alias Giri, a bladesmith in a remote village near Thirupaachi, has a lovable sister Karpagam and runs into hilarious incidents, while searching a groom for his sister. When a city guy proposes to Karpagam, Giri accepts their relationship. Giri accompanies the newlyweds to Chennai.
  3. Don
    • Year: 2022
    • Main Actors: Sivakarthikeyan,priyanka Mohan,S.J.Suryah
    • Description:The film revolves around a reluctant engineering student repeatedly clashing with his college's discipline mentor, while still trying to discover his passion in life and prove his eligibility to his father.
  4. Namma veetu pillai
    • Year: 2019
    • Main Actors: Sivakarthikeyan,Anu Emmanuel,Aishwarya Rajesh
    • Description: A brother who dotes on his sister is forced into a situation where he has to get her married to a ruffian with whom he is at loggerheads, and it is unclear if their relationship will survive.
  5. Sandakozhi 2
    • Year: 2018
    • Main Actors: Vishal,Keerthy Suresh,Rajkiran
    • Description: After the events following the first film, Balu returns to Theni district after 7 years from USA to meet his family, where he learns about his distinct relative Pechankarasi "Pechi". Pechi's husband was killed by the villagers for killing a lower-caste person.
