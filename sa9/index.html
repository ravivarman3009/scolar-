Mahatma Gandhi

Better known as the Mahatma, or great soul, Gandhi was an Indian lawyer who led his country to freedom from British colonial rule in 1947. He was assassinated months later at age 78. Gandhi is most famous for his philosophy of nonviolence that has inspired civil rights leaders around the world.

Mahatma Gandhi And Indira Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi was an ally and friend of Indira Gandhi's father, Jawaharlal Nehru. Indira married Feroze Gandhi (no relation to Mahatma Gandhi) in 1942, and she changed her name from Indira Nehru to Indira Gandhi. Feroze died in 1960, and Indira raised their teenage sons Rajiv and Sanjay to be leaders to succeed her.
